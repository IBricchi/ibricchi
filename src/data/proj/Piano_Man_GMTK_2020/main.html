PianoMan was my submission to the GMTK Game Jam for 2020. This was the largest purely online game Jam in history with over 18k participants, and over 5k sumbissions all developed in under 48 hours. It was my first time building a full game (if a game jam game can be considered a "full" game) with the Unity Game Engine. The game jam theme was:

Out Of Control

I took this theme and interpreted it in terms of how music makes people loose control. It was a really great experience and I'm very much looking forward to pariticpaiting in more game jams and hackathons in the future.

One plays as a musitian with a piano. Enemies react based on the music that is being played, but you can take over with your own music. When you play the enemies who hear you play turn on eachother and act accordingly based on the song that you play. With this power you reach the end of each level and make a choice. These choices are then used to generate a unique 4 part composition based on your journey.
